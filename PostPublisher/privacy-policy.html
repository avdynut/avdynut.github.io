PostPublisher ("we", "us", or "our") provides a tool that allows users to publish posts to Meta platforms (such as Facebook and Instagram) via Meta APIs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use PostPublisher.

1. Purpose of the Application

PostPublisher is used exclusively for publishing posts to Meta platforms. The application does not provide social networking features, messaging, or unrelated data processing.

2. Information We Collect

Information from Meta APIs

When you connect your Meta account and grant permissions, we may access:

  • Basic profile information (e.g., name, user ID).
  • Pages or business accounts you manage.
  • Content you choose to publish (text, captions, media URLs).
  • Post publishing status and related metadata.

We request only the permissions necessary to publish posts on your behalf.

Technical Information

  • IP address and device/browser information.
  • Log data related to publishing actions and errors.

3. How We Use Information

  • To publish posts to your connected Meta accounts.
  • To authenticate and maintain your connection to Meta APIs.
  • To store publishing logs and troubleshoot issues.
  • To maintain application security and prevent abuse.

4. Data Sharing

  • With Meta: Data required for publishing is transmitted to Meta through official Meta APIs.
  • Service Providers: Hosting and infrastructure providers may process data strictly to operate the service.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process.

We do not sell personal information.

5. Data Retention

  • Access tokens are stored only as long as needed for publishing functionality.
  • Publishing logs may be retained for operational and security purposes.
  • Data is deleted upon account removal, subject to legal obligations.

6. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data by emailing privacy@yourdomain.com with the subject Data Deletion Request.

You may also remove PostPublisher from your Meta account settings to revoke access permissions.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including secure HTTPS communication and access controls.

8. Children's Privacy

PostPublisher is not intended for individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction).

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
