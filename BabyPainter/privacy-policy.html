Privacy Policy for BabyPainter

Effective Date: 2025-03-19

At BabyPainter, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Our app is designed to provide a fun and safe experience for children to draw and learn colors and objects. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal Information: BabyPainter does not collect any personal data such as names, email addresses, or location information.
  • Device Information: We do not collect any information related to your device, such as IP addresses or device identifiers.

How BabyPainter Works

BabyPainter is a simple drawing app where users can select colors or objects to draw, and the app will speak the name of the object. This app does not require any login or sign-in.

The app does not track or save any data about users' activities or drawings.

Third-Party Services

BabyPainter does not use any third-party services such as analytics, advertising, or external libraries that may collect or share user data.

Security

Since the app does not collect any personal data, no sensitive data is stored or transmitted. We aim to provide a safe environment for children while using the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy occasionally. If we make any significant changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed.
